Core Capabilities

Four pillars of interview intelligence, working in harmony.

Resume Intelligence

Upload a PDF resume and instantly extract skills, projects, and experience using LLM-powered parsing.

Live Transcription

Real-time speech-to-text via Deepgram, streaming every word from the interview directly to your dashboard.

Smart Follow-ups

AI generates probing questions based on what was said vs. what the resume claims. Never miss a gap.

Credibility Scoring

Cross-reference transcript claims against the resume to score candidate credibility and flag inconsistencies.

Three simple steps to AI-powered interview intelligence.

Get Insights

AI generates follow-up questions, consistency reports, and credibility scores automatically.
